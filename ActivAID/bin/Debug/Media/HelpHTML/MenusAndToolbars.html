Menus and Toolbars

The menus and toolbars in the ActivATE IDE utilize many of the commands and icons found in most Microsoft applications and are therefore very intuitive and easy to use.  They provide access to the commands necessary to open, save and edit files, debug test programs, access help files, change the visibility of windows within the IDE, etc.  Although the items in the toolbars can also be accessed from the menus or shortcut keys, they represent the most commonly used functions and their presence in the toolbars means quicker, more convenient access for the user.

To change the visibility of the toolbars, right-click anywhere in the toolbar/menu area at the top of the application to show the toolbar menu.  Toolbars shown with a checkmark are currently visible.  Click on a toolbar to toggle its visibility.

To move a toolbar to a new location, place the mouse over the vertical dashed line at the left edge of the toolbar (see picture below).  When the mouse changes to the move cursor click and drag the toolbar to its new location. 

 

Tip:  Many of the menus and toolbars are not available until a test program or file is opened within the application.
